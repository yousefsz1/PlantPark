## Frequently Asked Questions

**How does plant identification work?**
Take or upload a photo of your plant, and PlantPark uses AI to identify the species, flag potential health issues, and check whether it's safe around pets and children.

**How accurate is the AI diagnosis?**
Our AI-generated results are a helpful starting point, but they can be inaccurate or incomplete. For anything involving toxicity, safety, or a plant's health, please verify with a qualified professional (vet, doctor, or horticulturist) before acting.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
On iPhone: go to Settings > [your name] > Subscriptions, select PlantPark, and choose Cancel Subscription.
On Android: open the Google Play Store app, tap your profile icon > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions, select PlantPark, and choose Cancel Subscription.

**How do I delete my account?**
Open the app, go to Settings, and select Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

**Why do I need to enable location access?**
Location is used only to check local rainfall data, so PlantPark can skip watering reminders after it's rained. It's never shared with advertisers.
