# Privacy Policy for PlantPark

**Last updated: July 16, 2026**

PlantPark ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Fixing House Ltd, is an AI-powered plant care and identification app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using PlantPark, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

## Information We Collect

**Account Information**
When you create an account, we collect your email address to manage your account, let you log in, and send you account-related emails (such as password resets).

**Photos**
When you scan a plant, we collect the photo you take or select. Photos are sent to our AI providers (see "How We Use Third-Party AI Services" below) to identify the plant, diagnose health issues, and check pet/human toxicity. Photos may be stored so you can view your scan history and favourites within the app.

**Location**
With your permission, we collect your approximate location to check local rainfall data, which lets us skip watering reminders after it has rained. Location data is only used for this purpose and is not shared with advertisers.

**Purchase Information**
If you subscribe to PlantPark Basic or Pro, our payment processor (RevenueCat, via the Apple App Store) processes your purchase. We receive information about your subscription status (active, tier, renewal date) so we can unlock the right features, but we do not receive or store your card details — those are handled entirely by Apple.

**Usage Data**
We collect information about how you use the app (such as which features you interact with, scan counts, and app performance data) to improve PlantPark and fix bugs.

**Identifiers**
We use a unique user ID (created when you sign up) to associate your data with your account.

## How We Use Third-Party AI Services

To identify plants and diagnose problems, photos you scan are sent to:
- **Google Gemini** (Google LLC) — primary plant identification and health diagnosis.
- **Anthropic Claude** — secondary toxicity verification for pet and human safety.

These providers process the image to return results to the app. We do not permit these providers to use your photos to train their models beyond what is required to return your result, in line with their respective business/API terms.

## Other Third-Party Services We Use

- **Supabase** — securely stores your account, plant records, and app data.
- **RevenueCat** — manages subscriptions and purchases.
- **Open-Meteo** — provides rainfall data based on your approximate location (no personal account data is shared with this service).
- **Resend** — delivers account emails (password resets, confirmations) on our behalf.

## Data Retention

We retain your account and plant data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from within the app, which permanently deletes your account data from our systems.

## Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can:
- Delete individual plants or favourites at any time in the app.
- Delete your entire account and associated data from the app's settings.
- Contact us at the email below for any other requests.

## Children's Privacy

PlantPark is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.
